Head of Design & Development (global)

ABOUT WMA

WMA partners with industry-leading clients, including Universal Music, Sony Music, Warner Music, Live Nation, Disney, Netflix and Amazon, to create impactful and cohesive digital campaigns. We drive genuine engagement by connecting brands, artists and their audiences across music, entertainment, lifestyle, and technology. WMA’s global team is highly experienced and collaborative with bases in London, New York, LA, Nashville and Singapore.

CLIENTS

Live Nation, Amazon Music, Netflix, Disney, Sony Music, Universal Music Group, Warner Music and more.

THE ROLE

We’re seeking a multi-talented and driven individual to lead and evolve the creative and design studio of WMA as the Global Head of Design and Development. This pivotal role requires someone with a strong background in agency and design environments, with a deep understanding of design needs with focus on music, film, entertainment and branding sectors.

Someone who has worked at the cross-section of music and entertainment with a proven track record, managing large and complex budgets, establishing and leading design teams and delivering creative content at a high level. The ideal candidate will bring a robust network of contacts and relationships across the music and entertainment industry including artists, label management, music platforms, film studios, streaming platforms and brands.

This is both a strategic and hands-on leadership role. You will be instrumental in identifying and unlocking new creative opportunities, securing high-value projects, shaping pitch-winning concepts, and championing the capabilities of WMA Studios within the industry. A proactive, outward-facing approach is essential – building relationships, spotting opportunities, and positioning the studio at the forefront of creative innovation.

This role will work closely with our creative teams including designers, animators, developers, editors across all our global locations.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

Growth & Commercial Leadership

– Drive the growth of the division by securing new creative and commercial opportunities.

– Leverage a strong industry network to unlock revenue across music, entertainment and lifestyle sectors.

– Lead senior-level pitches and negotiations with strategic, creative and commercial authority.

– Develop compelling proposals and oversee budgets to ensure profitability and long-term value.

– Partner with senior leadership to shape and deliver the studio’s growth strategy.

Creative & Strategic Leadership

– Define and lead WMA Studios’ global creative vision across Design & Development.

– Oversee all creative output, ensuring exceptional standards, innovation and cultural relevance across music, entertainment and lifestyle.

– Lead end-to-end project delivery across formats and disciplines, from brief to execution.

– Drive a progressive, collaborative creative culture across the studio and wider agency.

Client & Industry Leadership

– Lead and grow high-profile global accounts, building trusted, long-term partnerships.

– Serve as senior creative lead in executive presentations and stakeholder negotiations.

– Champion and promote WMA Studios’ capabilities across the industry.

Team & Operational Leadership

– Lead and inspire a global, multidisciplinary Design & Development team.

– Oversee resource planning and operational efficiency across markets.

– Identify and onboard top creative talent and collaborators.

– Work closely with Film & Photography, Social, Marketing, PR and Partnerships to deliver an integrated, cross-functional creative offering.

SKILLSET

– Commercially minded with strong financial and budgeting acumen.

– Exceptional pitching and presentation skills with executive presence.

– A strong advocate for the commercial value of design, confident in selling creative excellence as a growth driver for clients.

– Deep understanding of production from pre- to post-production.

– Strong eye for quality creative direction and cultural relevance.

– Highly organised with the ability to manage multiple complex workstreams.

– Confident negotiator and relationship builder.

– Entrepreneurial mindset with a proactive approach to opportunity creation.

– Passion for music, entertainment and lifestyle culture.

EXPERIENCE

– Senior-level creative leadership experience within an agency or creative studio environment.

– Proven track record of generating new business and contributing to revenue growth.

– Established network within music, entertainment and lifestyle industries.

– Significant experience leading global, multidisciplinary creative teams

– Strong experience in pitching and presenting in high-profile, high-pressure environments.

– Experience managing large-scale productions and complex multi-stakeholder projects.

LEADERSHIP TRAITS

– Collaborative and inclusive leadership style

– Innovation-led thinking

– Entrepreneurial drive

– Operational efficiency

– Clear and confident communication

– Integrity, trust and respect

– Continuous curiosity and learning mindset
